Let’s take a moment to setup your mood buddy & support network

Mood Buddy

Your Mood Buddy is a vital way of letting the most important people close to you know how you are feeling during the day. Mood Buddy helps them reach out to you when your feeling low and provide the comprehensive support you need.

To connect with your buddy, they too will need to have an active Dragonfly account installed on their device.

Support Network

Your support network are your friends & family that you want to reach out to for support when you’re ready. No Mood Buddy logs will be sent to this support group.

You can edit all your Support Network details under your setting tab in your dashboard.
